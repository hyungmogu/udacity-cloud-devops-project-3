# Benefits of CI / CD

## Ideas

- 1. Introduction
    - The shenanigans of doing business without CI/CD
        - Have you ever been faced with the following problems?
            - Trying to implement new features, but it's taking too much time?
            - Developers fighting about overwriting on top of another's code?
            - Facing issues about only one developer knowing how to deploy code? 
            - Fearing changes because of the cost of what if it doesn't go right? 
            - Insecurity about incurring costs from unused resources?
            - Feeling nausiated over issues regarding server crashes and performance?
        - CI/CD to the rescue!
            - CI/CD brings automation to code checkings and deployment  
            - It's a level up in development process that frees time for developers to work on more features
            - Less trouble for the company as it grows into a larger organization
            - It's important in today's business environment whether it's big or small

- 2. Body
    - Adopting CI/CD is a great solution!
        - a. In terms of creating revenue
            - Faster and More Frequent Production Deployments
                - Business Term: New value-generating features releasaed more quickly
            - Deploy to production without manual checks
                - Business Term: Less time to market
        - b. In terms of protecting revenue
            - Automated Smoke Tests
                - Business Term: Reduced downtime from a deploy-related crash or major bug
            - Automated Rollback Triggered by Job Failure
                - Business Term: Quick undo to return production to working state
        - c. In terms of controlling costs
            - Catch Unit Test Failures
                - Business Term: Less bugs in production and less time in testing
            - Detect Security Vulnerabilities
                - Business Term: Prevent embarrassing or costly security holes
            - Automate Infrastructure Creation
                - Business Term: Less human error, Faster deployments
        - d. In terms of reducing costs
            - Automate Infrastructure Cleanup
                - Business Term: Less infrastructure costs from unused resources
            - Catch Compile Errors After Merge
                - Business Term: Less developer time on issues from new developer code

- 3. Conclusion
    - Thank you so much
